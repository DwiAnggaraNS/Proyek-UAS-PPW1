Our main features:

Comprehensive Product Management

Easily and efficiently manage all your product information. This feature allows you to upload and update product data, including descriptions, prices, categories, and stocks. Our system ensures that all your product information is neatly stored and quickly accessible whenever needed.

Detailed Supplier Database

This feature enables you to store and manage information about all your store's suppliers. You can add contact details, addresses, and other relevant information for each supplier. With a comprehensive supplier database, you can easily track and contact your suppliers to ensure smooth business operations.

Stock In and Stock Out Management

Our system provides efficient Stock In and Stock Out management to streamline inventory control. The Stock In feature allows real-time recording of incoming inventory, including details such as receipt date, quantity, and supplier, preventing stock shortages and ensuring product availability. The Stock Out feature manages and records outgoing inventory, capturing details like date, quantity, and destination. Accurate tracking aids in analyzing expenditure patterns and better stock management, ensuring a balanced and organized inventory system.

Flexible Data Export Options

Our Export Data page offers versatile options to export your inventory data in various formats. You can generate detailed reports, share information, or maintain records by exporting data as PDF, CSV, Excel, or printing it directly. For quick data transfer, easily copy data to your clipboard and paste it into other applications. These features streamline managing, sharing, and analyzing your inventory data, ensuring smooth business operations.
